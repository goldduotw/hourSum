About Hour Sum

The Architecture of Focus

Hour Sum was developed by Gold Duo to solve a specific friction point in professional development: tracking the long-term accrual of "deep work" hours. Unlike standard stopwatches that focus on seconds and minutes, Hour Sum is designed for projects that span months or years, such as preparing for the IRS Enrolled Agent (EA) credentials or completing complex numerical modeling projects.

How It Works

  • Persistent Tracking: The app utilizes local browser storage to ensure your progress isn't lost if you close the tab or restart your computer.
  • Decimal Precision: Many professional billing and certification requirements use decimal hours (e.g., 1.25 hours). Hour Sum calculates this automatically to two decimal places.
  • Picture-in-Picture (PiP): By detaching the timer into a floating window, you can keep your progress visible while navigating study materials or tax software without sacrificing screen real estate.

About the Developer

The lead architect at Gold Duo holds a Ph.D. in Atmospheric Science and has decades of experience in numerical modeling and technical consultancy. After successfully navigating the 2026 tax season as an Intuit Tax Associate, the need for a streamlined, "no-nonsense" hour tracker for tax professionals became clear. Hour Sum is the result of that intersection between scientific precision and practical tax-compliance needs.
