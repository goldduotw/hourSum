# hourSum
hours accrued (e.g., in exam prep)

It will be something like a stopwatch. I click 'start' and it starts counting time lapsed when I start studying the exam materials. When I am going to take a break, I hit a 'pause' button, and the clock stops. It resumes when I am back, and hit the 'resume' button. The exam prep is estimated to take 200-300 hours. so we dont really need to count to the seconds.

With most of the apps I found, they all have the format of HH:MM:SS. With Hour Sum, we'll have DD:HH:MM and at the bottom corner of it, we show a number of total hours with 2 decimal points.

When I click 'Start', the clock will reduce to a small corner display, showing just the current total hours (and perhaps a title of my choice, e.g., EA prep hours). The clock will not reset even if the computer 'sleeps'.
