Optimizing Your Enrolled Agent Prep

Preparing for the IRS Enrolled Agent (EA) exams requires a significant commitment, typically ranging between 200 to 300 hours of focused study. Managing this time effectively is the difference between passing and falling short.

Why Track Decimal Hours?

Most professional certification logs and tax industry standards utilize decimal hours (e.g., 1.5 hours instead of 1 hour and 30 minutes). Hour Sum handles this conversion in real-time, allowing you to copy-paste your exact study metrics directly into your professional logs.

Persistent Tracking Technology

Our tool utilizes local browser synchronization to ensure your data remains safe. Because we use localStorage, your accumulated time is preserved even if your browser crashes or your computer enters sleep mode during a long study session.
